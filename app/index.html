Safe Work Practices

1. At Core Custodian (CC Wallet), we prioritize the security of your wallet and personal information. Please be aware that we do not store the passphrase or pin you use to protect your wallet. It is essential that you keep this passphrase and pin secure and remember it, as recovery is not guaranteed if it is lost.  However, we advise against using our app on mobile devices with root access for enhanced security.

For the best protection, we recommend writing the passphrase down on paper rather than relying on digital methods like screenshots or photos, which can be susceptible to accidental loss or theft.
